New Approach to Education nationaleis

Education nationaleis a private primary school founded in 1994, located in Riadh Ennaser 1, refers to the process of facilitating learning and development in children. It encompasses the formal and informal methods employed to impart knowledge, skills, values, and attitudes to young learners. The goal of education for kids is to nurture their intellectual, social, emotional, and physical growth, preparing them for future challenges and responsibilities.
